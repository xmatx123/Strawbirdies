About Us

In the labyrinthine depths of the digital realm, amidst the ceaseless flux of pixels and code, there exists a curious duo, bound not by the shackles of convention but by the elusive allure of wanderlust. Jana and Mattis, ephemeral voyagers of the soul, embarked upon an odyssey across the vast expanse of the Americas, tracing a path from the bustling streets of the USA to the untamed wilderness of Patagonia.

Hailing from the lands of Deutschland, they emerged from the monochrome monotony of corporate life, seeking solace in the kaleidoscopic tapestry of the world. In the corridors of commerce, Mattis, a sage of numbers and strategy, once toiled amidst the labyrinthine algorithms of a bank, navigating the treacherous currents of product management. Meanwhile, Jana, with the fervor of an alchemist, delved deep into the arcane realms of Non-Profit Management, weaving intricate webs of compassion and empathy.

But amidst the cacophony of deadlines and quotas, they heard the siren song of adventure, beckoning them to forsake the familiar confines of routine and embrace the unknown. For what is life, if not a fleeting waltz across the stage of existence, where the only currency of true value is the richness of experience?

In the chronicles of time, Mattis, a youthful seeker of knowledge at 28, finds solace in the symphony of football matches, the cartographic wonders of Geography, the camaraderie of a well-crafted beer, and the simple pleasures that adorn the canvas of daily life. Jana, a luminous spirit of 27, finds delight in the culinary delights of great vegan cuisine, the vibrant tapestry of diverse cultures, and the serendipitous encounters that illuminate the path of discovery.

Together, they traverse the labyrinth of highways and byways, not as mere travelers, but as pilgrims in search of the ineffable essence of existence. For in the words of the enigmatic Kafka, "Paths are made by walking", and in each step, they forge their own narrative, a testament to the boundless spirit of human endeavor.
